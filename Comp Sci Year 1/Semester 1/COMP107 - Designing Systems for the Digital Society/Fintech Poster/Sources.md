## Strengths/Weaknesses

Gives insight into how the business began and where they are now. 
Klarna makes money through merchant commission, interest rates on purchases bought with finance, and late fees.
Klarna has two interdependent customer segments. Consumers and Merchants. It offers consumers convenience and merchants wider access to these consumers. It has helped certain platforms increase their conversion rates by 40%.
It also offers consumers risk reduction, though a "Pay after delivery" service. Klarna actually assumes the risk on both sides as merchants also receive payment immediately.
