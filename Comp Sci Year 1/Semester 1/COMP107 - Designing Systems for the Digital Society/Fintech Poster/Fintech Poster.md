### Overview
Klarna is an industry leader in the Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) market. It's an app that is used by millions and promises to let users "Pay [Their] Way", allowing them to access more flexible payment options.

### Who Uses Klarna
Klarna caters to both consumers and merchants. It offers consumers convenience and some risk protection, through it's pay on delivery option, and offers the merchants increase performance. It's helped platforms like "wish.com" and "fitnessguru" increase their conversion rate by 40%.
### Strengths
Flexible Payment Options-More options makes purchasing things more convenient for consumers, can can increase conversion rates for merchants.
Risk Protections-Klarna's "Pay On Delivery" feature decrease risk for consumers when ordering online, at no risk to the merchant as Klarna

### Weaknesses
Payment default by users. 
Klarna offers risk protections by taking on risk itself. This can mean that it could be put in jeopardy if there are a large amount of payment defaults by its users.

Potential for misuse.
While BNPL services can increase flexibility, there is potential for them to be misused by desperate or financially illiterate people. 
### Ethical Considerations
Encouraging overspending. 
Due to users being able to defer payments, it's possible many users may feel encouraged to spend more than they are able to pay back.

Debt Traps.
High interest rates, and poor understanding around what these services actually are can lead to vulnerable people accumulating debt and struggling to pay it off.

Transparency of Terms.
The other ethical problems with this business model can be exacerbated by poor transparency of terms. In particular Klarna's marketing as "Risk Free" option can mislead consumers into making poor decisions. 
### Opportunities
Global Expansion
Klarna currently operates in 26 countries, mostly throughout Europe. While this is a significant number it still leaves room for further expansion into other countries and regions such as India, although after expansion they will be competing with existing BNPL players in these spaces.

Diversification of Services & Integrating AI.
Klarna has the opportunity to provide a wider array of services, not just focused on payment. Such as helping users track budgets. It can leverage new advancements in tech such as AI, to achieve this.
