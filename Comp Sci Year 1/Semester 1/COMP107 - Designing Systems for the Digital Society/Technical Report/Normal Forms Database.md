## 1st Normal Form
- All data values must be atomic. No arrays or sets.

## 2nd Normal Form
- Is in 1st Normal Form
- All attributes depend on the whole primary key. 
- (Only really important for tables with composite primary keys)

## 3rd Normal Form
- Is in 2nd Normal Form
- There are no transitive dependencies within the relation. 
- Basically there are no attributes that are part of the relation that depend on a foreign key that is also part of the relation
